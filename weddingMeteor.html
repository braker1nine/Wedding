Gratitude

We would like to thank the important people who have made possible our journey here and those who will help us get to March 23rd.

  • Castleton Farms

    The location of our rehearsal dinner, wedding, and reception. Darla Walker has a reputation and she lives up to it. She's been a wonderful blessing when it comes to making our wedding day a dream.

  • Texture Photo

    Carrie Jo (and Jess) may be the photographers for the wedding, but that doesn't begin to describe their importance to us. They are best friends, confidants, and deeply trusted sisters in Christ. When Chris gave Lauren "the letter", Carrie Jo was the first person each of us talked to.

  • Brilliant Earth

    They worked with Chris (and the help of Lauren's favorite designs) to make a custom ring just for us. It was beautiful and we love the work Brilliant Earth does to give back to the communities where they get their diamonds.

  • Stanley's Greenhouse

    The site of one of Chris and Lauren's first (non) dates. They were so wonderful to let us shoot engagement photos in the greenhouse.
